Maths and Excel

At times when AI has more and more influence on our daily lives, coding becomes an essential skill to learn. This Excel course provides children with the knowledge needed to solve maths problems with the program's help. They will learn to analyse data, create charts and tables and use logic algebra. At the end of the course, children will carry on their investigation and present the project about the chosen topic.
