About Me

My name is Nicol Troyan; I teach mathematics and physics and create workbooks, training courses, games and quests. I am, first of all, a person who passionately loves to learn, children and teach. In my ideal world, children do not get an education. They keep exploring forever. They do not stop asking many questions, continue questioning the most diverse beliefs, and do not concentrate on polar points dividing the world into black and white. I dream that children look at the world curiously and enjoy studying it. In my lessons, I cultivate clear and straightforward communication of thought, and "silly" questions and doubts become a natural part of the lesson.

  • over 10,000 lessons taught
  • more than 100 students
  • 7+ years of teaching
  • on average, two grades up in the time of 1 year

I am originally from Latvia and moved to the UK at the age of 15; I know firsthand the difficulties of adapting to a new environment. I love to work with teenagers because they are dynamic, impetuous and very authentic. They live through a time of vulnerability, fatigue and growth, and my job is to support them through this time. In my lessons, I create a space where they can be themselves. Letting them know that I believe in them makes it easier to set challenging tasks. I believe and make them believe in the reality of their achievement. With young children, especially with intense work, the mood and inner feelings of the child play a key role. When a warm, trusting relationship has been built between us, identifying errors is not perceived as criticism but becomes support and help in moving forward. I strive for each lesson with a young child to find interesting, engaging materials and games. It is an active and lively teacher who engages in the work best.
